DYNAMIC QUALITY FRAMEWORK

The TAUS DQF Application is a tool to evaluate translation quality. Specifically, TAUS DQF "aims to provide a neutral environment and workflow to evaluate translated content generated by both translators and machines," while helping to "measure return-on-investment, measure productivity enhancements, and benchmark performance." (TAUS DQF Application).

I was one of the three developers at Spartan Software who were tasked to develop the application. I have linked below the publicly available youtube videos that demonstrate some of DQF's functionality - take a peek!

More information can be found at TAUS Website: www.evaluation.taus.net/tools
